Famous Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for forts, palaces and vibrant culture.

Kerala

Famous for its serene backwaters, houseboats, and lush green landscapes.

Goa

Goa is well known for its beaches, parties, and Portuguese architecture.
