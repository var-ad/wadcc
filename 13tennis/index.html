Tennis Rules

  • Matches are played in best-of-three or best-of-five sets.
  • Each game starts at 0 (love), then 15, 30, 40, and game.
  • Players must win by two clear points to win a game.
  • Serve alternates between players each game.
  • Ball must land inside service box on serve.
